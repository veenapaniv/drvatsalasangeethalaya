About Us

My Music Career

Dr. Vatsala earned her Ph.D. for her "Music in South Indian Inscriptions", a work that has unraveled complexities of music and culture. Right from the age of eight, Vatsala has taken to music, training in Hindustani music under Guru Anuradha Deshpande, Guru. Iswar Gadibidi. Under the guidance of Gurus: Dr. Sarvamangala Sankar, Dr. Hamsini Nagendra, Dr. B.M. Jayashree and others, D. Vatsala perfected the nuances of Carnatic Music. Building on the blessed knowledge. Vatsala has developed her own distinctive methodology for imparting Sangeeta training. The result is Dr. Vatsala's Sangeetalaya, a reputed musical institution in KR Puram, Bangalore that offers in person and online music lessons.
